## Overview

The Vevhu Enterprise design system is engineered for the high-stakes environment of real estate and land verification. The brand persona is **authoritative, meticulous, and resilient**. It balances the energetic urgency of land acquisition with the sober reliability of corporate governance.

The aesthetic follows a **Corporate Modern** style with a focus on high information density. It prioritizes clarity and efficiency over decorative flair, utilizing ample whitespace to make complex datasets legible. The UI evokes a sense of "digital infrastructure"—sturdy, organized, and unmistakably professional.

## Colors

The palette is rooted in warm amber tones and sophisticated neutrals:

- **Primary (#F59E0B):** Warm amber for primary actions, highlights, and brand identity
- **Primary Light (#FCD34D):** Light amber for backgrounds and subtle highlights
- **Primary Dark (#D97706):** Dark amber for pressed states and emphasis
- **Success (#22C55E):** Green for verified, synced, and active states
- **Warning (#F59E0B):** Amber for pending, idle, and caution states
- **Error (#EF4444):** Red for errors, flagged items, and destructive actions
- **Background (#FAFAF9):** Warm light gray for page backgrounds
- **Card (#FFFFFF):** Pure white for card surfaces and content containers
- **Muted (#F5F5F4):** Light gray for secondary text and borders
- **Foreground (#1C1917):** Near black for primary text and headings

## Typography

The typography system uses Inter for all text levels, ensuring consistency and readability:

- **Display Large:** 32px, Bold (700) - Dashboard titles and major headings
- **Display Medium:** 24px, Bold (700) - Section headers
- **Heading 1:** 20px, Bold (700) - Card titles and page headers
- **Heading 2:** 18px, Semi-Bold (600) - Subsection titles
- **Body Large:** 16px, Regular (400) - Primary content text
- **Body Medium:** 14px, Regular (400) - Secondary content text
- **Label:** 13px, Semi-Bold (600) - Button text and labels
- **Caption:** 12px, Medium (500) - Helper text and metadata

## Layout

The layout follows a mobile-first approach with responsive considerations:

- **Padding:** 16px standard, 8px compact, 24px spacious
- **Card Border Radius:** 12px for modern, approachable feel
- **Button Border Radius:** 8px for balanced proportions
- **Spacing Scale:** 4px, 8px, 12px, 16px, 24px, 32px
- **Max Content Width:** 480px for mobile, 1440px for desktop

## Shapes

- **Cards:** 12px border radius with subtle border and shadow
- **Buttons:** 8px border radius with clear hit areas (minimum 44x44px)
- **Inputs:** 8px border radius with clear focus states
- **Badges:** 12px border radius for pill-shaped status indicators
- **Avatars:** Full border radius for circular profile images

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do:
- Use clear, hierarchical typography for information density
- Maintain consistent spacing and alignment
- Provide clear status indicators for all data states
- Use brand colors purposefully for actions and emphasis
- Ensure touch targets are at least 44x44px for mobile

### Don't:
- Overuse decorative elements or animations
- Mix multiple primary colors in a single view
- Use shadows excessively—favor tonal layering
- Hide critical actions behind multiple taps
- Use low-contrast text on light backgrounds
